September 20, 2019

First of all, not as good as last year.

I had high hopes going into this rally.

It was pretty much the entire school watching the seniors have fun. They did the seating assignment in such a way that the football team got the attention. Not a very good start for this year's sensei.

That was a painful 45 minutes for me. Talking to friends afterwards, we concluded that the rally was actually bad.
