The Dinner Dance - May 11, 2018

This was a pretty fun night for me, except for the hour leading up to it.

That hour was filled with crazy moms taking pictures.

Now to the dance.

About 155 kids went.

First we checked in with Mrs. Tappin, my 8th grade science teacher.

Then we ate meatballs and pasta for dinner.

And then most of us danced (there were like 20 who decided Fortnite was what they wanted).

Dancing = jump up and down screaming the song lyrics.

I did that most of the time, and then for like 5 minutes I started doing that thing where I start moving my feet around really fast.

Everyone started chanting my name (this is the exception to the Invisible Ben thing).

And now this is why it took me 2 months to put out this story.

It's all in this next sentence.

I got kissed my a girl.

I felt nervous to type that.

Yes, a girl named Molly came right up to me and tried getting me on the cheek, but instead got my around the ear.

And this happened like an hour into the dance (like half an hour after we all started dancing).

I then sat out for half an hour (and I regret doing that).

My fame was over, but not the dance.

I went back out for the last hour and a half for the dance.

And then I went home.
