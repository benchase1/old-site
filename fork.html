Fork

Albums made by only the best people that run the main website. Fork entered planning stages on November 7, 2018 when Ben's friend showed him an image of a plastic fork on the ground. After months of delays, Fork released on April 14, 2019. The albums are also available for streaming on SoundCloud and Spotify.

Usage

Anyone is free to use the Fork tracks for any personal use. It can be a school project, personal hobby, or even as a relaxing beat. It's free to use! However, anyone using the tracks for a commerical, money-making use is only allowed to do so with written permission. This includes content creators on YouTube and Twitch who make money from their respective platform. Written permission can be obained by emailing fork@benchase.tk with your channel or organization details.
