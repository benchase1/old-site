Usage

Anyone is free to use the Fork tracks for any personal use. It can be a school project, personal hobby, or even as a relaxing beat. It's free to use! However, anyone using the tracks for a commerical, money-making use is only allowed to do so with written permission. This includes content creators on YouTube and Twitch intending to monetize their content by placing ads. Written permission can be obained by emailing fork@benchase.tk with your intended use of the tracks.
