YouTube Channel

My YouTube channel began on January 17, 2014 when I uploaded a video of me playing Minecraft. Between then and August 25, 2017, I gained 73 subscribers and nobody watched my videos (max 5 views each). I deleted the channel after pressure from family. I recreated the channel on September 1, 2017 to create better content, and have since gained 89 subscribers and over 1,500 views.

Various friends of mine have been featured in my videos.
